# Do Not Merge To Production

Do not copy Studio source directly into production `src/`.

Do not treat mock data as live.

Do not treat localStorage founder mode as security.

Do not treat simulated source/referral/claim UI as deployed.

Do not treat prototype DEX/LP/toolkit links as verified unless sourced.

Do not treat burn/fire totals as real unless backed by chain truth.

Do not treat recognition/leaderboards as rights, rewards, or governance.

## Production Readiness Rule

A Studio idea becomes production only after Codex creates an approved, tested, truth-grounded integration slice with real data boundaries, release guards, and founder approval.
