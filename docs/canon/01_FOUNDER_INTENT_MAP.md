# 01 — FOUNDER INTENT MAP

For every major concept: **why it exists**, **what problem it solves**, and **what
it must never become**. This is the "why" layer — the guardrail against scope creep
and drift. Mechanics live in code; values live in `02_SOURCE_OF_TRUTH_TABLE.md`.

> Governing intent: *The Syndicate is a society, not a security.* The product is
> identity and belonging, verifiable on-chain. Meaning precedes mechanics.

## The non-negotiables (apply to every concept above)

- **No financial rights.** SYN, ranks, and artifacts confer no ROI, yield,
  dividend, profit share, equity, redemption, or governance-by-spend. Ever.
- **Recognition ≠ return.** Spending more buys *recognition* (rank, archive
  placement), never *rights or returns*. This must be stated plainly, never implied away.
- **Code outranks docs.** Every public claim maps to an on-chain read or is
  labeled PENDING.
- **Cohort > rank, always.** Identity (the seat) and belonging (the chapter)
  outrank the mutable recognition layer (rank).
