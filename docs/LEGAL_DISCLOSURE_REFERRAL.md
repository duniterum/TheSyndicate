# Referral — Legal Disclosure Doctrine

**Status:** Doctrine. Public disclosure block lives at the bottom of `/referral`.

## Required public language

The Referral page MUST carry, at minimum, the following statements:

1. Referral, if activated, is an Operations-slice commission on protocol membership sales.
2. Any commission is paid from the **Operations slice only**. Vault and Liquidity allocations are never touched.
3. Participation in the referral program does not constitute a security, investment contract, or promise of profit.
4. Local laws may require additional disclosure when sharing your referral link.
5. The Syndicate is an experimental utility membership protocol; participation may result in total loss.

## Banned copy on the Referral surface

- "Passive income", "yield", "ROI", "guaranteed", "earnings projection".
- Implied appreciation of SYN as a result of referring.
- Tier names that imply rank-based revenue ownership, passive income, or guaranteed payout.
