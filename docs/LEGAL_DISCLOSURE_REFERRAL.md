# Referral / Source Attribution - Legal Disclosure Doctrine

Status: NON-LIVE DISCLOSURE DOCTRINE / ONE PAUSED INTERNAL SOURCE

Public disclosure must reflect the current V3 truth:

- SourceRegistryV1 is deployed with one internal PAUSED source record.
- Public V3 buys use `ZERO_SOURCE_ID`.
- No source-aware public link is active.
- No referral/source commission is accruing.
- No claim UI is live.
- No displayed source balance exists.

## Required Public Language

The Referral page MUST carry, at minimum, the following statements:

1. Referral/source attribution is not live until a source is activated, read
   back, legally approved, and wired into an approved source-aware buy path.
2. Source attribution, if activated, is acquisition attribution on protocol
   membership sales, not ownership of a member relationship.
3. Any acquisition commission must be visible in the V3 receipt as
   `acquisitionCost`; the remaining net USDC routes 70% Vault, 20% Liquidity,
   and 10% Operations.
4. Participation in a future source program does not constitute a security,
   investment contract, yield product, passive-income product, or promise of
   profit.
5. Local laws may require additional disclosure when sharing a future source
   link.
6. The Syndicate is an experimental utility membership protocol; participation
   may result in total loss.

## Banned Copy On The Referral Surface

- "Passive income"

- "ROI"
- "guaranteed"
- "earnings projection"
- "downline"
- "MLM"
- "claimable commission" while claim UI is inactive
- implied appreciation of SYN as a result of referring
- tier names that imply rank-based revenue ownership, passive income, or
  guaranteed payout

## Activation Carve-Out

Before public source/referral activation, legal/product signoff must approve:

- source class and source terms,
- commission language,
- whether source terms apply to one purchase, a window, caps, or custom scope,
- payout/escrow wording,
- no-yield/no-passive-income/no-member-ownership disclaimers,
- failure and pause/revoke states,
- the exact public route copy.

Until reviewed, `/referral` remains noindex and pending.
