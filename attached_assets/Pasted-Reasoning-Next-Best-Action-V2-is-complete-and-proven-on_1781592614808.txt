Important model

Connect / Enter ≠ Membership.

Header actions must separate:

1. Connect
   Meaning:
   connect wallet / future login.
   Not membership.

2. My Syndicate
   Meaning:
   open dashboard/cockpit.
   Not membership by itself.

3. Join The Syndicate / Become a Syndicate Member
   Meaning:
   go to Sale V2 membership purchase flow.

4. Buy More SYN
   Meaning:
   member-only continuation of same Sale V2 action.
