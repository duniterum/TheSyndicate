PART 4 — REFERRAL CASH FLOW CLARITY
──────────────────────────────

This must be explained and implemented clearly.

Operations wallet is a normal wallet, not a smart contract.

Referral payment must happen inside the same buy transaction.

Example:

Buyer pays 10 USDC with a valid referrer.

Without referral:

* 7 USDC → Vault
* 2 USDC → Liquidity
* 1 USDC → Operations

With referral:

* 7 USDC → Vault
* 2 USDC → Liquidity
* 0.50 USDC → Referrer
* 0.50 USDC → Operations

Important:
The 1 USDC Operations slice is split before final routing.
It is NOT sent to Operations first.
Founder does NOT manually pay referrer later.
Referrer receives automatically in the same transaction.

Flow should be:

Buyer wallet
→ Sale V2 contract
→ Vault / Liquidity / Referrer / Operations

NOT:

Buyer
→ Operations
→ Referrer

Please update the architecture/report/code comments so this is impossible to misunderstand.

Questions:

1. In the current draft, at what exact step does the referrer receive USDC?
2. Does Sale V2 pull USDC into the contract first and then fan out?
3. Does the Operations wallet ever need to pay the referrer manually?
4. What happens if referrer transfer fails?
5. Is referral escrow safe and clear?
6. Does the event show referralAmount and operationsAmount separately?
