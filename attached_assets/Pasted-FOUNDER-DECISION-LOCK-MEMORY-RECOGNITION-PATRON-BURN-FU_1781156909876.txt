## PART 2 — FOUNDER DECISION PROPOSAL

Convert these founder directions into clean doctrine proposals:

1. Chronicle
   Not every event becomes Chronicle.
   Chronicle is curated memory.

2. Recognition
   Recognition is not KYC.
   Recognition is not verified real-world identity.
   Recognition can be anonymous, alias-based, or self-declared later.

3. Patron
   Patron Seal = artifact-backed support / recognition.
   Patron rank, if kept, must always be disambiguated.
   Never use bare “Patron” when ambiguous.

4. Proof of Fire
   Proof of Fire = SYN sent to the standard dead address.
   Founder Burn / Community Burn are classifications.
   Ash Seal = future artifact concept only.

5. Referral
   Referral is future attribution first, payout later.
   Example future memory:
   “Member #27 brought Member #456 into The Syndicate.”
   No live commission language until contract exists.

6. Reports
   Weekly Chronicle, Monthly Report, Quarterly Report, Half-Year Report, Annual State of The Syndicate are future outputs derived from events + metrics + ledger.
