SPRINT A.7 — RANK NAMING LOCK + FINANCIAL TRACE GUARDRAILS

This is an execution sprint with careful doctrine alignment.

Goal:
Apply the final rank rename decisions, reserve Patron for support, and clarify how money-related data may be used without turning The Syndicate into a simple wealth leaderboard.

Founder correction:
Money data is not shameful. It is part of the protocol truth.

The Syndicate should show:

* how much SYN a member bought
* how much USDC was routed through their actions
* how their purchases affected Vault / Liquidity / Operations
* how they helped protocol growth
* how their journey evolved over 1 month / 1 year / 10 years
* what they can verify on-chain

But money alone must not create the highest historical prestige.

Money can create:

* rank
* financial trace
* participation depth
* member journey history
* proof of support
* protocol impact reporting

Money must not automatically create:

* Chronicle inclusion
* historical-first status
* top Signal tier
* governance rights
* yield / ROI language
* guaranteed recognition

## TASK 3 — FINANCIAL TRACE DOCTRINE

Add/align doctrine:

Financial Trace = verifiable history of a member’s economic participation.

It can include:

* cumulative SYN purchased
* cumulative USDC routed through sale
* purchases over time
* rank history
* NFT mints
* burn participation
* referral-attributed volume later
* protocol wallet impact
* yearly member journey summary

It is valuable because it is verifiable.

It should help users see:

* “I was here.”
* “I participated.”
* “I helped the protocol grow.”
* “Here is my on-chain proof.”

But it must be clearly separated from:

Historical Prestige:

* member number
* firsts
* Chronicle inclusion
* Proof of Fire
* chapter sealing
* meaningful signals

---

## TASK 4 — SIGNAL GUARDRAIL REFINEMENT

Do NOT ban money-derived data from the system.

Instead enforce:

Money-derived data may feed:

* Rank
* Financial Trace
* Member Journey
* Reports
* Ledger
* Activity
* Participation-depth signals

Money-derived data must NOT by itself:

* raise an event above S2
* create S3/S4/S5 historical signal
* create Chronicle publication
* create governance implication
* create reward/ROI wording

Example:
A $10,000 purchase can be shown clearly.
It can produce rank recognition and financial trace.
But it should not outrank:

* Member #7
* Proof of Fire #001
* first artifact mint
* chapter sealed
* Chronicle inclusion

Size can be visible.
Size can motivate.
Size can tell a story.
Size cannot be the only reason something becomes historical.
