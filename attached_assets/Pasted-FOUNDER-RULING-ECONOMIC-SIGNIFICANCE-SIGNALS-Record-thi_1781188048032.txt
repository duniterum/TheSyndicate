FOUNDER RULING — ECONOMIC SIGNIFICANCE & SIGNALS

Record this ruling in canon.

No Signal Engine implementation yet unless explicitly requested after this.

Goal:
Clarify the doctrine before Sprint B so the Signal Engine does not underestimate money while also avoiding wealth-leaderboard drift.

---

## FOUNDER RULING

Money is not suspicious.

Money is one of the core truths of The Syndicate.

Without money:

* no members
* no Vault
* no Liquidity
* no Operations
* no protocol growth

Economic activity is proof that the machine is alive.

The protocol should show economic activity proudly, truthfully, and verifiably.

However:

Money alone must not automatically create permanent Historic status or Chronicle publication.

---

## CORE DISTINCTION

Economic significance is real.

But the SUBJECT decides standing.

Protocol-subject economic milestones may become high-tier Signals.

Person-subject money alone remains Financial Trace / Participation Depth / Visibility unless combined with non-money facts.

---

## RULE A — PROTOCOL-SUBJECT ECONOMIC MILESTONES

Protocol-subject economic milestones may become S3+.

Examples:

* Vault threshold crossed
* Liquidity threshold crossed
* USDC routed milestone
* SYN sold milestone
* chapter sealed
* protocol crossed a major economic level

These are about the protocol, not a person.

They may become:

* Signal candidates
* Chronicle candidates
* Report highlights

Guardrails:

* thresholds must be pre-declared
* no retroactive threshold invention
* no buyer attribution in Chronicle wording
* no price/ROI/yield claims

---

## RULE B — PERSON-SUBJECT MONEY

Person-subject economic activity may be visible and important.

It may create:

* Financial Trace
* Participation Depth
* rank recognition
* Activity rows
* Report context
* Member Journey history
* magnitude marker
* support/participation context

But money alone must not automatically create:

* Historic status
* Chronicle publication
* permanent prestige
* governance implication
* reward/ROI language

A large purchase is meaningful.
It can motivate others.
It can signal confidence.
It should be visible.

But it is not automatically immortal.

---

## RULE C — MAGNITUDE MARKER

Approve a magnitude marker concept.

Purpose:
Surface large economic events without turning them into permanent prestige.

Examples:

* largest purchase in 24h
* largest purchase this week
* major purchase event
* high participation event

Rules:

* magnitude marker is not a Signal Tier
* it does not live in signal-registry.ts
* it must not feed Chronicle automatically
* it must not feed Recognition permanently
* it must not become a top-spender leaderboard
* it is temporary visibility / Activity / Report context

---

## RULE D — REPEATED COMMITMENT

Repeated buying or continued participation over time may be recorded as Participation Depth.

It should NOT be called Conviction.

Conviction remains reserved for irreversible or high-commitment actions such as:

* Proof of Fire
* burn events
* future irreversible support actions

Avoid investment-performance language such as:

* held through drawdown
* bought the dip
* survived market crash
* adverse window

---

## RULE E — SIGNAL ENGINE IMPACT

Sprint B should not suppress economic significance.

But signal-registry.ts must remain money-safe.

Do not add amountUsd, usdcAmount, synAmount, walletBalance, archiveWeight, builderScore, commissionUsd, commissionPct to StructuralFacts.

Instead:

* protocol thresholds enter as pre-declared structural facts
* person-subject money remains Financial Trace / Participation Depth
* magnitude marker stays outside Signal Tier logic
