## REFERRAL VISION, BUT DO NOT IMPLEMENT CONTRACT

Referral should be modeled as attribution first, payout later.

Future event example:

“Member #27 brought Member #456 into The Syndicate.”

If the referred member buys through the sale:

* record who brought whom
* record buyer/member number
* record USDC routed
* record SYN sold
* mark reward status as PENDING unless future contract exists

This should feel like:

* service to the Syndicate
* verified growth contribution
* member recognition
* future reward infrastructure

But it must NOT imply live commissions until the contract exists.

Forbidden:

* “earn now”
* passive income
* ROI
* yield
* guaranteed reward

## BURN REQUIRED FIX

The founder already performed burn transfers to the standard dead address.

Ensure:

* Burned Supply = SYN.balanceOf(dead address)
* burn event count supports multiple burn transfers
* Proof of Fire #001, #002, etc. are event-derived
* Activity can show Burn events
* Chronicle can treat burn events as candidates
* no one-off hardcoded burn card only

Use wording:

* verified supply reduction
* standard dead address
* Proof of Fire
* Founder Burn
* Community Burn

Do not use:

* buyback
* price impact
* pump
* ROI
* yield
