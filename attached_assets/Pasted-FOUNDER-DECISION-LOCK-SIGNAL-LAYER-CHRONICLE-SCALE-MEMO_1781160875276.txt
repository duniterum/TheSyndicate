## PART 2 — DEFINE SIGNALS

Define “Signal” for The Syndicate.

Proposed definition:

A Signal is a classified protocol event that indicates timing, conviction, support, growth, memory, or structural significance.

Examples:

* Member Number signals timing.
* Rank signals depth of participation.
* Patron Seal signals support.
* Proof of Fire signals conviction.
* Referral attribution signals growth work.
* Chapter sealing signals protocol progress.
* Chronicle inclusion signals historical importance.

Clarify:

* Signal is not a new token.
* Signal is not a reward.
* Signal is not KYC.
* Signal is not financial rights.
* Signal is not governance.
* Signal is a prioritization and meaning layer.

## PART 5 — CHRONICLE SCALE RULE

Define the scalable Chronicle rule:

At small scale:

* founder/operator can manually review candidates.

At medium scale:

* only S3+ signals enter weekly review.

At large scale:

* only S4+ signals enter Chronicle review.
* S2/S3 appear in reports and recognition surfaces.
* S0/S1 stay Activity-only.

Chronicle should never become a raw feed.

Chronicle publication remains:

* CANDIDATE
* APPROVED
* PUBLISHED
* REJECTED

No auto-publication without approval.

## PART 6 — RECOGNITION SCALE RULE

Recognition should be action-based, not identity-based.

Default display:

* Member #N

Future optional displays:

* alias
* public self-declared name
* image/avatar

Important:
No KYC.
No “verified identity.”
No promise of truth about real-world identity.

Recognition should answer:
“What meaningful signals has this member emitted?”

Examples:

* Early
* Patron Seal holder
* Proof of Fire participant
* Chapter sealer
* Growth builder
* Historic member
* Founder-era participant

Do not implement paid identity/profile now.
Only define the scalable recognition model.

## PART 7 — REFERRAL AS SIGNAL

Referral is future only.

Define it as:

Growth attribution first.
Reward payout later.

Future example:

“Member #27 brought Member #456 into The Syndicate.”

Signal logic:

* normal referral = S1/S2
* 10 successful referrals = recognition candidate
* referral that brings milestone member #333/#1,000 = Chronicle candidate
* referral reward status remains PENDING until contract exists

No live commission language.
No “earn now.”
No passive income.
No guaranteed reward.
